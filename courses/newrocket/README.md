# newRocket — Forward Deployed AI Engineer

Interview preparation for the Forward Deployed AI Engineer role in newRocket's Agentic AI department. Covers ServiceNow platform fundamentals, AI/LLM engineering on ServiceNow, the forward deployed engineering model, and company-specific context.

> **Perspective**: You're an experienced full-stack engineer with AI/LLM knowledge. This course focuses on the ServiceNow-specific platform knowledge, enterprise AI delivery patterns, and client-facing engineering skills that differentiate this role from a standard backend or ML engineering position.
>
> **Prerequisite**: [LLM Engineering](../llms/) course and [General Interview Techniques](../general-interview/)
